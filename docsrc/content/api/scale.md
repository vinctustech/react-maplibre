ScaleControl
============

A component that adds a [ScaleControl](https://docs.mapbox.com/mapbox-gl-js/api/markers/#scalecontrol) to a map.

#### `position` 'top-right' | 'top-left' | 'bottom-right' | 'bottom-left' *(optional)*
